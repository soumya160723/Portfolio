About Me

Hi, I'm Soumya Erra. I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Cyber Forensics and Information Security at Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad.

Although I began this journey with no background in coding or computer science, I’ve developed a strong passion for cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and digital investigations. My curiosity about how cybercrimes are committed — and more importantly, how they can be detected and prevented — drives my motivation to keep learning.

I'm currently building my foundation in:

  • Python programming
  • Linux commands and shell scripting
  • Networking basics (IP, DNS, ports)
  • Digital forensics and system analysis
  • Web application security (OWASP Top 10)

My goal is to become a cybersecurity analyst or digital forensics expert. I'm working hard to build real-world skills through hands-on labs, projects, and certifications like TryHackMe and Hack The Box. I created this portfolio to showcase my growth, projects, and learning milestones.

Outside academics, I enjoy reading cybercrime case studies, watching tech documentaries, and experimenting with beginner-level tools and scripts.
